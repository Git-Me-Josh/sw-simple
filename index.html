How To Sign

REGISTER TO VOTE

All supporters who wish to have their voice heard must be registered to vote for their signature to count. If you haven't registered to vote, you can do so online HERE . Once you've registered, you can sign and support the initiative!


Option 1: Download a single-signature E-Sheet with full text.

Do you support the effort to expand Sherwood's public safety and police funding with the taxation of sales of marijuana in Sherwood? Click here to download and print an Electronic Signature sheet (along with the full text of the initiative). Complete all six sections -- two dates, two signatures, your printed name and your address in Sherwood (it must be the one you use when you registered to vote).

To have your voice heard, we've got to receive the original Electronic Signature Sheet back from you! You can:

  • Drop it by Western Oregon Dispensary in Sherwood or Newberg, OR
  • Mail it to:
  • Sherwood of Tomorrow Committee
  • 15025 SW Tualatin-Sherwood Rd
  • Sherwood, OR 97140

Do you support the effort to expand Sherwood's public safety and police funding with the taxation of sales of marijuana in Sherwood? Click here to read the full text of the Sherwood of Tomorrow ISHE2020-1 petition. This petition is designed to be easily read and understood, so citizens know exactly how this will support our community:

  • Any proposed marijuana business would only be allowed in Sherwood's industrial zone on the way out of town toward Tualatin and farm from schools, residential areas and shopping areas.

  • Every single cent of tax revenues generated by the safe and secure marijuana sales would go directly to Sherwood public safety and the Sherwood Police Department.
