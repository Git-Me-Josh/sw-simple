How To Sign

REGISTER TO VOTE

All supporters who wish to have their voice heard must be registered to vote for their signature to count. If you haven't registered to vote, you can do so online HERE . Once you've registered, you can sign and support the initiative!

Option 1: Download a single-signature E-Sheet with full text.

Do you support the effort to expand Sherwood's public safety and police funding with the taxation of sales of marijuana in Sherwood? Click here to download and print an Electronic Signature sheet (along with the full text of the initiative). Complete all six sections -- two dates, two signatures, your printed name and your address in Sherwood (it must be the one you use when you registered to vote).

To have your voice heard, we've got to receive the original Electronic Signature Sheet back from you! You can drop it by Western Oregon Dispensary in Sherwood, Western Oregon Dispensary in Newberg, or mail it to: Sherwood of Tomorrow Committee, 15025 SW Tualatin-Sherwood Rd, Sherwood, OR 97140.

E-Sheet

Option 2: Download the 10-signature circulator sheet and collect signatures.

Do you support the effort to expand Sherwood's public safety and police funding with the taxation of sales of marijuana in Sherwood? Click here to download and print an Petition Signature sheet (along with the cover page and the full text of the initiative). You are empowered to collect as many signatures as you can! Remember, all signers needs to be registered to vote and be a Sherwood city resident (zip 97140). You should sign the first line with your own signature, date signed, printed name and address (the same one you used when you registered to vote). You can collect up to 9 more signatures on this same sheet (and if you need to, you can collect more on another sheet!). Once you're done collecting signatures, you must also sign the Circulator Certification at the bottom. Please include your name as the Circulator, and include your signature, the date signed, your printed name and your address. You do not need to fill in the "Sheet Number" line -- we'll take care of that one when the signatures are submitted!

To have your voice heard, we've got to receive the original Electronic Signature Sheet back from you! You can drop it by Western Oregon Dispensary in Sherwood, Western Oregon Dispensary in Newberg, or mail it to: Sherwood of Tomorrow Committee, 15025 SW Tualatin-Sherwood Rd, Sherwood, OR 97140.
