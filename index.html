Why Yes?

  • Any proposed marijuana business would only be allowed in Sherwood's industrial zone on the way out of town toward Tualatin and far from schools, residential areas and shopping areas.

  • Every single cent of city tax revenues generated by the safe and secure marijuana sales would go directly to Sherwood public safety, supporting items such as:
    • Crosswalk safety lighting
    • Community patrol in parks and public areas
    • Portable speed units in neighborhoods

  • And more! Read the initiative and contact us if you have any questions -- we are here to fight dishonest misinformation and help our fellow citizens understand how removing the ban will help Sherwood.
